# electrostatic_field.md

## 1. FIELD DEFINITION
Electric field is generated only by source charges.

## 2. FIELD ORIENTATION
Electric field direction is the direction of force on a positive test charge.
- Positive source: field points away.
- Negative source: field points toward source.

## 3. FIELD VECTOR
E_vec = (k*q_source*d_vec)/r**3

## 4. VECTOR SUM
Never directly add field magnitudes unless collinear AND directions resolved. Otherwise, resolve components and sum vectors.

## 5. MIDPOINT CASES
Opposite charges at a midpoint often reinforce. Do not assume cancellation automatically.

## 6. STANDARD UNIT AND CONSTANTS
Use: V/m
- Coulomb constant: ke = 9e9
