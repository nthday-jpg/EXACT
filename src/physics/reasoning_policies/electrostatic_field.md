##1. FIELD DEFINITION
Electric field is generated only by source charges.

##2. FIELD ORIENTATION
Electric field direction:
direction of force on a positive test charge.

Positive source:
field points away.

Negative source:
field points toward source.

##3. FIELD VECTOR
E_vec = (k*q_source*d_vec)/r**3

##4. VECTOR SUM
Never directly add field magnitudes unless:
- collinear
AND
- directions resolved

Otherwise:
- resolve components
- sum vectors

##5. MIDPOINT CASES
Opposite charges at midpoint often reinforce.

Do not assume cancellation automatically.

##6. STANDARD UNIT
Use:
V/m
