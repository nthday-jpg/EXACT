##1. DC POWER
P = U*I

Equivalent forms:
P = I**2 * R
P = U**2 / R

##2. AC AVERAGE POWER
P = U_rms * I_rms * cos(phi)

##3. PURE RESISTOR
For pure resistors:
cos(phi) = 1

Thus:
P = U_rms * I_rms

##4. REACTIVE ELEMENTS
Ideal inductors and capacitors consume zero average power.

Average power:
P_avg = 0

##5. RESONANCE
At resonance:
- phase angle = 0
- cos(phi) = 1
- average power is maximal

##6. POWER FACTOR
cos(phi) =
R / Z

##7. JOULE HEATING
Heat energy:
Q = I**2 * R * t

##8. ENERGY VS POWER
Power:
rate of energy transfer

Energy:
E = P * t

##9. RMS VALUES
AC power formulas use RMS values unless explicitly stated otherwise.
