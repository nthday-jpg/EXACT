##1. TOTAL IMPEDANCE
Z = sqrt(R**2 + (XL - XC)**2)

##2. PHASOR GEOMETRY
Voltage across:
- R is in phase with current
- L leads current by +90°
- C lags current by -90°

##3. SERIES VOLTAGE
U_total**2 =
VR**2 + (VL - VC)**2

##4. SEGMENT IMPEDANCE
Z_segment =
sqrt(R_segment**2 + (XL_segment - XC_segment)**2)

##5. SEGMENT VOLTAGE
U_segment = I * Z_segment

##6. ORTHOGONAL PHASORS
If voltages are 90° out of phase:

U_total**2 =
U1**2 + U2**2

##7. PHASE RELATION
tan(phi) = (XL - XC)/R

##8. IMPEDANCE VS VOLTAGE
Impedances combine by circuit structure.

Voltages combine by phasor geometry.

##9. RESONANCE
Resonance cancels TOTAL reactance only.

Do not cancel local segment reactances.
